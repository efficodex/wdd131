Santos Emprendedores in Lima

Site Plan

Site Name

Santos Emprendedores in Lima represents a platform that highlights the entrepreneurial efforts of members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Lima, Peru. The name emphasizes the focus on the local community and the spiritual identity of its participants.

Suggested domain: santosemprendedoreslima.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a digital space that promotes both temporal and spiritual self-reliance among members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Lima. The site aims to:

  • Explain the concept of self-reliance from a gospel-centered perspective.
  • Show how spiritual principles support personal economic progress.
  • Highlight small businesses and entrepreneurial initiatives by local members.
  • Foster support networks and connections among member entrepreneurs.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find businesses run by Church members in Lima to support with my purchases?
  • How can I register my business on this platform if I am a member of the Church?
  • What does the Church teach about self-reliance and how does it relate to personal business efforts?
